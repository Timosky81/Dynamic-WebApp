About

Dynamic People's Association (DPA) is a self-help organization that draws its membership from Salary earners and business owners who pool money together for financial intermediation and investment opportunities that provide benefits to its members. It raises funds by obtaining loans, investments and savings from members to further its objectives.

DPA aims to attract people with common vision who desire a safety net during emergencies and economic growth to meet their aspirations.
